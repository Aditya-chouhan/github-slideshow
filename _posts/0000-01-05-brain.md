## NEXUS Brain — The Master Orchestrator

<div class="two-col">
<div class="col-left">

The **NEXUS Brain** is a meta-agent that sits above all 35 specialized agents. It sees everything, routes everything, resolves conflicts, and learns what works.

**What it does:**
- Routes tasks to the right agent across any terminal
- Resolves conflicts when agents have competing priorities
- Learns from outcomes — gets smarter about YOUR business
- Prioritizes by business goals, not just urgency
- Triggers cross-terminal cascades from a single instruction

**Example:** *"Prepare for board meeting"* triggers Finance Agent (deck), KPI Agent (metrics), HR Agent (headcount update), Sales Agent (pipeline summary) — all coordinated by the Brain.
