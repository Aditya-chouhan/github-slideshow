## Building the Future

<div class="roadmap">

<div class="phase" style="border-left-color:var(--nx-accent);">
<div class="phase-tag">Phase 1 — Foundation (Months 1–4)</div>
<h3>Core Terminals + Agent Infrastructure</h3>
<p>Launch Sales, CRM, and Finance terminals with 15 agents. Claude integration in every terminal. Trust Dial (Levels 1–3). Basic cross-terminal communication. NEXUS Docs & Sheets. Org hierarchy with role-based views.</p>
</div>

<div class="phase" style="border-left-color:var(--nx-accent2);">
<div class="phase-tag" style="background:var(--nx-accent2);">Phase 2 — Full Platform (Months 5–8)</div>
<h3>All 7 Terminals + Agent Mesh + Memory</h3>
<p>Add Marketing, Dev, HR, and Ops terminals (35 total agents). Full Agent Mesh with autonomous cross-terminal workflows. Organizational Memory Engine. Level 4 autonomous mode. NEXUS Meet, Comms, Boards, Sign, Schedule. Proactive Business Health Monitor.</p>
</div>

<div class="phase" style="border-left-color:var(--nx-green);">
<div class="phase-tag" style="background:var(--nx-green);">Phase 3 — Industry Verticals (Months 9–14)</div>
<h3>Vertical AI + Marketplace + Scale</h3>
<p>Industry-specific agent templates (SaaS, e-commerce, healthcare, fintech). Agent marketplace — third-party agents that plug into the Mesh. Advanced analytics with time-travel analysis and cross-company benchmarking. Enterprise features: SSO, SCIM, audit logs, on-prem deployment. API platform for custom integrations.</p>
</div>
