## Claude AI — Inside Every Terminal

<div class="two-col">
<div class="col-left">

Every terminal has a **Claude chat interface** — Anthropic's most powerful AI, embedded in context.

**Not a chatbot. A co-worker.**

- **Sales Terminal:** *"Draft a follow-up for the Acme deal — they went dark 5 days ago"* — Claude pulls full CRM history, writes the email, suggests optimal send time
- **Finance Terminal:** *"How did Q1 compare to forecast?"* — Claude queries all financial data, generates a CFO-quality summary in plain English
- **Dev Terminal:** *"Why did build #847 fail?"* — Claude reads logs, traces the root cause, suggests a fix with a PR draft

Claude doesn't just answer questions — it has **full context** of every agent's activity, every data point, every decision in that terminal.
